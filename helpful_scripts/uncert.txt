WToTauNu:
taues_X (shape) : tau energy scale seperately for the three decay modes X (2%)
FR_XX (shape) : fake factor uncertainties in Bin XX ()
BkgdNorm (lnN) : background normalization for true taus (MC) (30%) 


WToMuNu:
mu (shape) : muon momentum scale (2%)
TTNorm (lnN) : tt normalization (15%)
EWKNorm (lnN) : EWK normalization (15%)
MuEff (lnN) : muon reconstruction efficiency (4%)


Both:
jes (shape) : jet energy scale
ues (shape) : unclustered energy scale
BinX_X (shape) : bin-by-bin uncertainties
lumi (lnN) : luminosity
